# Privacy Policy for Vocab Flash

**Last Updated:** December 28, 2024

## Overview
Vocab Flash ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection
Vocab Flash stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

- **Learning Progress:** Number of words you've learned
- **Streak Data:** Your daily learning streak count
- **Current Word Index:** Which word in the sequence you're currently on
- **Last Visit Date:** The last date you used the extension

## Data Storage
All data is stored **locally on your device only** using Chrome's local storage. We do not:

- Collect any personal information
- Send data to external servers
- Track your browsing history
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Share your data with third parties

## Data Usage
The data we store locally is used solely to:

- Track your learning progress
- Maintain your daily streak
- Resume where you left off
- Provide you with new vocabulary words

## Data Control
You have complete control over your data:

- All data is stored on your device only
- You can reset your progress at any time by uninstalling the extension
- Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all stored data

## Third-Party Services
Vocab Flash does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy
Vocab Flash does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

## Consent
By using Vocab Flash, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
